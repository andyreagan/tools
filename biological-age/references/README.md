# Reference Materials — Biological Age Calculator

Papers, spreadsheets, and analysis notes collected while building the
PhenoAge biological age calculator at `tools.andyreagan.com/biological-age/`.

## Papers

### PhenoAge (the model we implemented)
- **levine2018-phenoage.pdf** — Levine ME et al. "An epigenetic biomarker of
  aging for lifespan and healthspan." *Aging* 2018;10(4):573–591.
  DOI: 10.18632/aging.101414. Open access.

### Aging.AI
- **aging-ai-v1-putin-2016.pdf** — Putin E et al. "Deep biomarkers of human
  aging: Application of deep neural networks to biomarker development."
  *Aging* 2016;8(5):1021–1033. DOI: 10.18632/aging.100968. Open access.
- **aging-ai-v3-mamoshina-2018.pdf** — Mamoshina P et al. "Population Specific
  Biomarkers of Human Aging." *J Gerontol A* 2018;73(11):1482–1490.
  DOI: 10.1093/gerona/gly005. PMC6175034.

### MassMutual LifeScore Med360
- **massmutual-med360.pdf** — Maier M et al. "Improving the Accuracy and
  Transparency of Underwriting with Artificial Intelligence." *AI Magazine*
  2020;41(3):78–93.

### Physical Activity & Mortality
- **fishman2016association.pdf** — Fishman EI et al. "Association between
  Objectively Measured Physical Activity and Mortality in NHANES."
  *Med Sci Sports Exerc* 2016;48(7):1303–1311.
  DOI: 10.1249/MSS.0000000000000885.

## Spreadsheet
- **pheno-age-calculator.xls** — PhenoAge calculator by John G. Cramer
  (jcramer@uw.edu), modified by Michael Lustgarten. Implements the Levine
  2018 formula with US-unit inputs and automatic conversions. Used to verify
  our JavaScript implementation.

## Analysis
- **MODEL-ANALYSIS.md** — Detailed notes on the Aging.AI model architectures,
  input parameters, min/max ranges from the archived website forms, training
  data descriptions, and performance benchmarks. Written during research for
  this tool.

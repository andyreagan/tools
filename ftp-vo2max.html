Note on accuracy
This calculator uses the ACSM metabolic equation [1], which was developed for lab cycle ergometry. Sitko et al. [2] measured FTP and VO₂max directly in 44 trained road cyclists and found a flatter relationship: VO₂max = 10.2 × FTP(W/kg) + 12.6. At typical trained values (~4 W/kg) this calculator estimates ~65 ml/kg/min vs Sitko's ~54 — roughly 20% higher. The gap likely reflects real-world efficiency losses not captured by the lab equation. Treat these estimates as approximate upper bounds.
